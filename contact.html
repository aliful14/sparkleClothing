Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product type. For labels, we typically require 100 pieces minimum, while hang tags start at 50 pieces. Contact us for specific requirements.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 2-3 weeks for most products. Rush orders are available for an additional fee. Custom designs may require additional time for approval and setup.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! We have an in-house design team that can create custom labels, tags, and packaging solutions tailored to your brand. We'll work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

What materials do you use?

We use premium materials including polyester, cotton, satin, and specialty fabrics for labels. Our hang tags are made from high-quality cardboard, kraft paper, and specialty papers.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Can I get samples before ordering?

Absolutely! We offer sample production for most products. This allows you to see and feel the quality before placing your full order. Sample fees apply but are credited toward your final order.
